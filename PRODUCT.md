# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo creators, indie hackers, and small marketing teams who post the same content across many social platforms (X, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.). They live in the dashboard: composing, scheduling, and reviewing posts quickly between other work.

## Product Purpose

Post Train is a social cross-posting and scheduling tool: write once, publish everywhere, on a schedule or via queue slots. Success = a post reaches every connected platform with minimal clicks and zero confusion about what goes out when.

## Brand Personality

Crisp, engineered, dependable — "transit system" energy in deep teal. The metaphor: your content rides a reliable line to every destination. Confident and utilitarian, not playful-pastel.

## Anti-references

- Post Bridge (post-bridge.com): the product this was derived from. Must NOT share its trade dress — no pastel green (#66cc8a), no soft rounded-pastel look, no diagonal-rails logo motif.
- Generic 2024 SaaS gradient-purple templates.

## Design Principles

1. **Transit-map clarity** — deep-teal line color, high-contrast states, unambiguous "what departs when".
2. **Flow is sacred** — layouts, IA, and interaction flows stay as-is; visual identity changes only.
3. **Engineered, not decorated** — tighter radii, subtle machined shadows, one accent color doing real work (Restrained color strategy: neutrals + cobalt accent).
4. **Boring reliability** — no motion or flourish that slows down a daily scheduling workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum: brand teal #0e8177 is 4.75:1 on white; on-primary text is pure white; the deep shade (#0a5f59, 7.5:1) carries small link text. Light theme only (no dark mode currently).
